Our Innovative Services

Home / Our Innovative Services
We proudly introduce two of our innovative services to explore:

We are the only consultancy that specializes in helping you dispose of non-functional, obsolete, and unusable equipment at the best possible price. Our team, include biomedical engineers and seasoned stores personnel, who conducts thorough assessments to determine usability and valuation, providing certification to ensure compliance with government guidelines.

We also offer comprehensive end-to-end condemnation services. Our expert consultants will lead hospitals through the entire process, from forming committees to identifying condemnable items—such as biomedical equipment, medical records, machinery, linen, and other general items— facilitating the tendering and disposal based on the committee's recommendations.

We are confident in our ability to streamline the condemnation process for healthcare organizations, ensuring it is completed efficiently, effectively, and in full compliance with all relevant regulations.

With extensive experience in the industry, we fully understand the challenges that hospitals (large or small) and individual doctors face when navigating legal services. The stress of multiple hearing dates can be both financially taxing and mentally overwhelming. Additionally, healthcare professionals often struggle to convey their professional details to lawyers.

Our team comprises medico-legal experts who are not only doctors but also practicing lawyers, providing you with the expertise needed to address these complexities effectively in the court-room.

*We adopt a unique pricing policy where charges are not per hearing rather a consolidated sum based on the complexity of the case.
