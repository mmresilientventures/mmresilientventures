Dr PK Tyagi

Home / About Us

MBBS, MD(Community Medicine); AFMC, Pune

Dr. PK Tyagi is a highly accomplished Public Health Specialist with over 20 years of robust experience in the Indian Armed Forces. His expertise lies in the strategic planning and design of community development programs, along with the development of impactful IEC strategies for diverse communities. Dr. Tyagi has expertly led outbreak investigations and efficiently managed logistics for healthcare facilities and medical colleges. His significant projects are centered on the prevention and control of both communicable and non-communicable diseases, including scrub typhus and mental health initiatives, showcasing his commitment to public health excellence
